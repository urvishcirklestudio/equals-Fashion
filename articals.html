Welcome to Equals: Our Story Begins

At Equals, our journey began in 2022 with the mission to provide affordable, high-quality products in a community-driven environment. Opening our first store by the seaside in Surat, India, we set out to redefine what shopping could be—a place where trust and equality come first. Our focus has always been on creating a seamless shopping experience where everyone feels valued, whether shopping online or in-store. Over the years, we’ve worked hard to build a reputation for quality, reliability, and customer service, growing our store into what it is today.

As we expand our product offerings, our commitment to transparency and ethical practices remains strong. Whether it’s fashion, home goods, or daily essentials, every item we carry is carefully selected to reflect our values of fairness and accessibility. We believe that high-quality products shouldn’t come at an unreasonable price, and that’s why we offer affordable, stylish, and sustainable options for everyone. We’re proud of how far we’ve come, but we’re even more excited for what’s ahead.
